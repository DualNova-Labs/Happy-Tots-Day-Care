About Happy Tots Day Care

At Happy Tots Day Care, our mission is to provide a safe, secure, and nurturing environment for your little ones. We focus on creating a space where children feel comfortable, loved, and encouraged to explore and learn.
With our highly qualified staff and child-centered approach, we ensure that each child receives individual attention and care tailored to their unique needs and developmental stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do your programs cater to?
Our programs are designed for children ages 0-6. We offer tailored learning experiences through our Little Explorers (0-2 years), Curious Minds (2-4 years), and Bright Scholars (4-6 years) programs.
How flexible are the schedules?
We offer flexible schedules to accommodate busy parents. Our programs provide both full-time and part-time options, allowing families to choose the times that work best for them.
Do you provide progress reports for children?
Yes, we provide regular progress reports for each child. These reports detail their development in key areas such as cognitive, social, and physical growth, ensuring parents are always informed about their child's progress.
What is the student-to-teacher ratio?
We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. For our younger age groups (0-2 years), the ratio is 4:1, while for older children (4-6 years), the ratio is 8:1.
Are your programs safe for infants and toddlers?
