About Happy Tots Day Care

We believe that every child has a unique potential waiting to be discovered. Our mission is to create a nurturing and stimulating environment that inspires a lifelong love of learning.
At Happy Tots Day Care, our mission is to provide a safe, secure, and nurturing environment where children aged 6 months to 3 years can thrive. Guided by our core values of TRUST, SECURITY, and TRANQUILITY, we truly are a "Safe Heaven For Your Little Ones".
